fshred 2.0 a tiny/robust recursive shredder
Unix-like

shred can be frustrating. It doesn't support recursive operations, and doesn't provide fine-grained control on its streams. Enter fshred. While supporting much of the base functionality of shred, fshred adds both recursion, and finer control on where and how much to shred.
